Spotting Morphed Faces – Key Points

  1. What is Morphing in Faces?
    • Face morphing is the blending of two or more face images to create a new identity that resembles all source images — commonly used to fool biometric systems.
  2. How Detection is Done?
    • Visual Artifact Analysis: Looks for inconsistencies like blur, ghosting, or unnatural textures.
    • Deep Learning-Based Detection: CNNs learn pixel-level anomalies that indicate morphing.
    • Landmark Inconsistency Analysis: Measures symmetry or mismatches in facial landmarks.
  3. Tools & Technologies Used
    • Image Processing: OpenCV, PIL, Scikit-Image.
    • Deep Learning: TensorFlow, PyTorch, VGG-based or ResNet architectures.
